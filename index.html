Eden Saig

I am a graduate student at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, advised by Nir Rosenfeld, and soon starting a postdoctoral appointment at Caltech. I am interested in machine learning and algorithmic decision making in social contexts. In particular, my research aims to develop socially-favorable learning algorithms for behavioral environments with dynamics and incentives.

Before my doctoral studies, I received my BSc (CS and Physics) and MSc (Theoretical Computer Science) from the Technion. I was lucky to be advised by Eli Ben-Sasson, and supported by the Lapidim undergraduate scholarship. After my MSc, I was a research scientist at the Facebook Core Data Science group.

My research is supported by the Israeli Council for Higher Education (CHE) scholarship for doctoral students in Data Science.
